Fair questions

Is this legal advice?

No, and we're upfront about that. Offsider is practical guidance built around the Fair Work way of doing things and the spirit of the Small Business Fair Dismissal Code. It helps you run a fair process and keep good records, but it's not a lawyer. For formal warnings or anything heading toward dismissal, we'll nudge you to get proper advice from an employment-law or HR adviser first.

Do I need HR training to use it?

Not at all. Offsider is built for owners and managers who've never done HR in their life. You type what's going on in plain words, and it walks you through the rest one step at a time. If you can describe the situation to a mate, you can use Offsider.

Is my information private and secure?

Yes. Your records are yours. Worker information is stored securely and only visible to the managers you give a login to. We don't sell your data, and you can export or delete your records whenever you want.

What if warnings are already underway?

No worries — you can jump in partway through. Tell Offsider what's happened so far, including any warnings or conversations, and it'll help you log them, work out where you're up to, and show you the next sensible step.

Can my whole team use it?

The Crew and Site plans come with several manager logins, so the people who manage staff can all work from the same tidy records. Everyone who needs to handle people decisions can have their own login and stay on the same page.
